About The Event

HackData is an annual 24-hour hackathon at the Shiv Nadar University. It's a place for students to learn new skills and come up with creative solutions using technology. To help along the way, we provide mentorship and workshops. Beginners are welcome!

Problem statement

Choose any one track from the following

Energy

Design an innovative technology or system that addresses the goal of decarbonizing the global energy system and stabilizing greenhouse gas emissions. Solutions may include a redesign or rethink of existing technologies and practices or entirely new strategies

Education

Educational technology aims at use of educational theoretic to facilitate learning among students and improving performance by using, and managing appropriate technological processes and resources. Make a solution which facilitates this and more.

Security & Resilience

How might wearable technologies, biometric sensors, and augmented reality provide enhance human capabilities and self/situational awareness? How might Blockchain technologies be applied to assure information exchanged is accurate and secure and future systems? Code away at solutions to a secure and resilient future.

Economic Development & Entrepreneurship

The “Make In India” initiative is a way to promote domestic entrepreneurship and economic development. It is the need of the hour for us to come up with ways to help this cause. So think hard, take out your creative hats and innovate technologies to be “The Start-up” or to help existing start-ups.

Smart City Solutions

A smart city is a city that incorporates various technologies which enhances the quality and performance of services such as energy, transportation and various other utilities. So hack your brains and think of ways to make our lives more efficient and easier.

F.A.Q

  • Is there a registration fee?

    NO! Registration is absolutely free!

  • What will be the selection criteria?

    We will review your github profile and send you the selection email a day or two after the registrations close.

  • What is the last date of registration?

    The deadline for registrations is January 27th.

  • Will food be provided?

    Yes, food is on us! Also expect yummy snacks to munch on while doing some hard core coding

  • Will accomodation be provided?

    Yes, accomodation will be provided free of cost

  • Will the cost of transportation be reimbursed?

    The cost of transportation will NOT be reimbursed. However, transportation from the nearest metro station to the campus will be provided

  • Will there be any activites to do during the hackathon?

    Yes, you can check out our annual cultural-techno-sports fest Breeze while you take a break! Visit this site to learn more! Breeze

  • What will I get out of attending the hackathon?

    Loads! There are prizes worth over 1 lakh INR to be won with a special prize for an all women's team. Apart from that, there are amazing attendee kits, tshirts, stickers and other cool swag to be collected.
